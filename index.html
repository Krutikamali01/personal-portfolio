KRUTIKA MALI

Ui/Ux Designer

Hello, I’m Krutika Mali! As a UI/UX Designer, my objective is to create intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital experiences that enhance usability and engagement. I focus on understanding user behavior, conducting research, and designing seamless interactions through wireframing, prototyping, and interaction design. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for innovation, I strive to bridge the gap between user needs and business goals. My goal is to contribute my creativity, problem-solving skills, and user-first approach to crafting impactful digital products in a dynamic and collaborative environment.
